About ReviewLift

The review funnel tool
for local UK businesses

ReviewLift is a reputation management platform that helps local businesses collect more positive Google reviews and handle unhappy customer feedback privately — before it ever reaches a public review site.

What is ReviewLift?

ReviewLift is a review funnel tool for local businesses in the UK. When a customer interacts with a business using ReviewLift, they are asked how their experience was. Satisfied customers are directed to leave a public review on Google, Trustpilot, Checkatrade, or any platform they choose. Dissatisfied customers are invited to submit private feedback that only the business owner can see — keeping complaints off Google.

Businesses can reach customers through four channels: a QR code placed on tables, receipts or a counter; a direct link shared anywhere online; an SMS message sent from the dashboard; or a branded email. All four channels feed into the same review funnel.

ReviewLift also includes an AI reply generator that produces polished responses to Google reviews in seconds, and a full analytics dashboard showing visits, ratings, feedback, and review click-through rates.

Why ReviewLift exists

Most local businesses know that Google reviews matter — they influence where customers choose to spend their money, how their business appears in local search, and how much trust they build over time. But collecting reviews is uncomfortable. Asking customers directly feels awkward. And when a bad experience happens, there's no way to catch it before it becomes a public one-star review.

The idea came from watching a family member's business get hit with a wave of 1‑star reviews from one difficult group of customers. Overnight, their hard‑earned reputation tanked. They had no way to intercept those complaints before they went public. That's why ReviewLift exists — to give every local business a chance to fix things privately, before a single bad day defines them online.

ReviewLift solves both problems. It makes it easy and natural for happy customers to leave a review — a QR code on the counter, a short text after their visit — while giving unhappy customers a private outlet that only the business owner sees. The result is a steadily improving Google rating and a private channel for handling complaints constructively.

What ReviewLift does — in plain terms

  • Review funnel page — A personalised landing page for your business that routes customers based on their experience.
  • QR code generator — Instantly creates a print-ready QR code that links directly to your funnel. Place it anywhere customers can see it.
  • Direct link — A short shareable URL for your funnel. Works in email signatures, social bios, or printed cards.
  • SMS requests (Pro) — Send a personalised text message to a customer's phone number directly from your dashboard. One tap on the link takes them to the funnel.
  • Email requests (Pro) — Send a branded review request email from your dashboard. The email includes your business name and a single clear call-to-action.
  • Private feedback capture — Unhappy customers write to you privately. You read it in your dashboard. It never appears on Google.
  • Analytics dashboard — Shows total visits, positive and negative splits, star rating distribution, review click-through rate, and all private feedback messages.
  • AI review reply generator (Pro) — Paste any customer review, get a polished professional reply written by AI in seconds.
