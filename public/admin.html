🔍 How to find your Google review link

Your Google review link is the direct URL customers use to leave you a review on Google. Here's how to find it in 60 seconds:

1
Go to Google Maps Open maps.google.com and search for your exact business name and location.
2
Click on your business listing It should appear in the left panel or on the map. Make sure it's your official Google Business Profile.
3
Click "Write a review" You'll see this button in your business profile panel. Click it — it'll open a review popup.
4
Copy the URL from your browser Copy the full URL from the address bar. It will look something like this:
https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=ChIJxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

Alternatively, log into your Google Business Profile, go to your profile, and look for "Get more reviews" — Google gives you a short shareable link there too.
