How it works

How does ReviewLift work?

Your funnel works wherever your customers are — QR, SMS, Email, or Link — separating happy and unhappy feedback instantly.

01
🏪

Create your funnel

Sign up, add your Google review link, and instantly get a unique funnel page and a printable QR code.

02
📲

Customers scan your QR

Place it on tables, receipts, or your counter. Customers scan it and answer one simple question.

03
✨

The funnel does the work

Happy customers go to your review page. Unhappy customers leave private feedback — only you see it.
