How it works

Three steps. Then it runs itself forever.

01
Connect your booking system

Paste one webhook URL into Calendly, Fresha, Booksy, Soe Exact, or any tool with Zapier. Takes 2 minutes. No developer needed.

02
AI writes the message

After each appointment, ReviewLift reads the service, the staff name, and the customer. It writes a message that sounds exactly like the business owner wrote it.

03
The funnel does the rest

Happy customers go to Google. Unhappy customers go to a private feedback form. You deal with complaints quietly, and only 5-star reviews reach the public. All automatically.
