Frequently asked questions

How does white-label work?
You get a branded dashboard URL (e.g., dashboard.youragency.com). Your logo and colours appear everywhere — clients never see ReviewLift branding. Your clients think you built the whole platform.
How do I get paid commission?
When a client signs up through your unique referral link, you earn 30% of their subscription every month. Payments are sent via bank transfer on the 1st of each month.
Can I add more than 10 clients?
Yes, contact us for custom enterprise pricing. We can scale with you as you grow.
Do my clients need their own accounts?
Yes, each client gets their own login under your agency dashboard. You control access and can manage everything on their behalf. They never see your agency's backend.
What's the difference between Agency and Pro?
Pro is for individual businesses managing their own reviews. Agency is for resellers who want to white-label the platform and manage multiple clients. Agencies get 30% commission on all client subscriptions.
